My background

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA. I have worked for many years on set for TV and film. However, working in tech has always been a dream. Recently, I have been getting closer to realizing my ultimate potential in this new field. I started learning HTML when I began modifying code on a template for my personal blog and website. I wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

Then while working on a crew of a motion capture film, I was able to collaborate with amazing artists and engineers on projects via Unreal Engine, Vlad 3D models, and Giant Software. I was in awe and completely hooked, and once again, I felt compelled to learn more. I find working in tech so liberating and empowering. I'd love to share my skills to help you create something meaningful. Let's be brilliant together!
